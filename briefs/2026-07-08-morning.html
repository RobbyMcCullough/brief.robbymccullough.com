Why Robby has standing: This is exactly the trust layer behind agentic websites: where agents are useful, where permissions get dangerous, and what operators need before clients should rely on them.

For website workflows, I would want narrow repo/site scopes, a staging-only default, human approval before public writes, and logs that a non-engineer can inspect.
